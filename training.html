Driver Training


Express EMS drivers are well-trained in first aid assistance and patient handling. We also provide them with training in basic skills like communication, grooming, technology, and safety protocols.
Ambulance driver training is crucial to ensure safe and effective transportation of patients. It includes defensive driving, emergency vehicle operations, traffic laws, communication, and patient handling. By investing in comprehensive driver training, ambulance services can improve the quality of care they provide to their patients and reduce the risk of accidents or incidents on the road.
